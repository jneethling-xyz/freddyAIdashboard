Transfer Rate / Handover Rate
❌ Invalid Transfers — Root Causes
  • Intent mismatch — Bot misread the query and served the wrong article or response (e.g. device name → setup steps, "phone call settings" → mobile app assumption)
  • No clarifying questions — Transferred without asking anything, leaving customer stuck and agent without context
  • Bot repeated itself — Looped the same response regardless of what the customer said
  • Wrong workflow triggered — Cancellation/complaint workflow fired incorrectly (e.g. "we don't need itemised billing")
  • Workflow not triggered / no handover message — Transfer happened with no context passed to the agent
  • KB article wrong or missing — Wrong article surfaced or no article existed to cover the topic
  • Persona / config — Bot made assumptions, didn't re-engage after unhelpful rating, or escalated too early
✅ Valid Transfers — Why They Were Right
  • Customer explicitly requested a human
  • Genuinely agent-only action (user removal, account changes, cancellations, DPA)
  • Customer tried the steps and still needed help
  • Sensitive topic or account issue requiring verification
  • Workflow correctly triggered (complaints, escalations)
Resolution Rate — Resolution Types
✅ Resolved: Bot answered via KB article
  • Genuine self-serve wins — bot served the right article and customer confirmed it helped
  • Examples: Yealink admin password, call redirect steps, DD setup, contract renewal info
  • Worth noting: some had no feedback clicked despite appearing resolved
⚠️ Auto-resolved: Inactivity / Drop-off
  • Customer stopped responding — could be resolved, could be abandoned
  • Hard to call true resolutions — no confirmation the answer actually helped
  • Issues hiding in here: bot responded in Malay/Indonesian, wrong email given, article marked unhelpful before drop-off
🚫 Auto-resolved: Out of Hours
  • Conversation closed because no agents were available — not a resolution
  • Several had unhelpful ratings — bot's answer wasn't good enough and customer had no fallback
  • Issues spotted: bot gave wrong email address (mycleaningfairy.co.uk), broadband and billing queries left unanswered
🔁 Handover: Workflow / Automation Triggered
  • Bot correctly identified a cancellation, complaint, or loyalty scenario and routed accordingly
  • Working as designed — not resolutions, but correct escalations
  • Cancellation workflows consistently triggering as expected
⚠️

Key concern: Inactivity and out-of-hours auto-resolves are inflating the resolution rate. A customer dropping off or being told to call back is not the same as being helped — and several of those rows have unhelpful ratings to prove it.

Post-Live Issues
🤖 Hallucinations
  • Invented download URLs → 404 errors
  • Gave wrong bOnline contact email (mycleaningfairy.co.uk)
  • Told customer it would add a new user — then never did
  • Gave steps to remove a user (feature doesn't exist)
  • Surfaced email address not in any article
  • Echoed customer's own email back as bOnline's contact
🔀 Intent / Context Misreads
  • "Waiting on response" → bot apologised for engineer visit
  • "OK" → bot asked about uploaded files (none present)
  • "YES" → bot gave full services overview unprompted
  • "mope" (typo) → bot asked to clarify the word "mope"
  • Customer gave personal details → bot treated as number transfer
  • "See previous chat" → bot gave invoice download steps
  • "Can you reopen it" → bot triggered cancellation workflow
⚙️ Workflow Misfires
  • Cancellation not triggering for: "close account", "i want to cancel", "i want to leave bonline", "cancel bOnline account"
  • Cancellation triggering incorrectly for: "change phone number", "we don't need itemised billing"
  • Rated unhelpful → customer could still chat instead of being transferred
  • Loyalty workflow not triggered for package downgrade request
  • Refund/complaint sent to cancellation workflow instead of agent
  • Out-of-hours cancellations not routing to workflow
🚪 Premature / Silent Transfers
  • Transferred on "hi" without responding
  • Transferred on "remove CRM integration" without attempting to help
  • Multiple cases: no context or summary passed to agent
  • Bot transferred to group instead of following correct workflow
  • Customer asked for invoice → straight to queue, no attempt to help
📄 Missing KB / Article Not Served
  • "Turn off voicemail" → no article, no response
  • "Update my bank details" → correct article exists but not served
  • "Change direct debit" → article exists, not triggered
  • "Change name on billing" → no article triggered
  • Call issues on device → no troubleshooting article served
  • Bot referenced further docs but never provided the link
💬 Contextual Limitations
  • Correct answer delivered as a wall of steps — agent said the same thing in one sentence
  • No clarifying questions before serving router article (assumed model)
  • Answered about extensions, ignored the actual request ("reconnect my number")
  • Looped name/email collection instead of progressing
  • Live vs AI Studio behaviour inconsistent for same query
🔧 Platform / Technical Bugs
  • Bot fallback error triggered multiple times after name/email provided
  • Bot responded in Malay/Indonesian after customer gave first name
  • Old "Chat with Support" widget still live post go-live
  • Handover summary not sent to agents (requires 10+ messages to trigger)
  • Analytics conversation ID filter not retained
  • Email capitalisation bug triggering wrong handover message
